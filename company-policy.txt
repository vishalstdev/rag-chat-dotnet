Contoso Corporation Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Eligibility: All full-time employees are eligible for remote work up to 3 days per week.

2. Equipment: Company will provide laptop and monitor. Employees responsible for internet connectivity.

3. Working Hours: Core hours 10 AM - 3 PM in employee's timezone. Flexible schedule outside core hours.

4. Communication: Daily standup at 10 AM via video call. Slack for async communication.

5. Performance: Evaluated on output and results, not hours logged.
